Solid white surface for anything with fine data — numbers, charts, medical records, forms. The non-glass counterpart to GlassCard.

```jsx
<Card>…weight chart…</Card>
<Card statusColor="var(--danger)">…overdue vaccination…</Card>
```

- Use over glass whenever the content has small text/numbers (legibility rule).
- `statusColor` adds the 4px left stripe used by health status cards.
